About Elegance

Elegance is a family owned restaurant that serves Italian food that has been passed down for generations. As a family we chose to open our restaurant in 1993. Our family, friends and community enjoyed our family recipes so much that we felt obligated to share such wonderful and taditional Italian food.


Not only is Elegance about family and tradition. We employ some of the areas top chefs. Elegance does not lose the art in plating and cooking. We maintain a balance of culinary art, tradition and flavor.
